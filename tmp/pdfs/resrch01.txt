3. Income Certificate (Government Service Pattern)
(Note: As this is a standard infrastructural service, the workflow is highly deterministic across most e-
District platforms).
1. Basic Information: State Revenue Department. Objective: To certify the annual income of a family
for educational or welfare purposes. 2. Eligibility: Any citizen can apply. The calculated income must
reflect the true earnings of the household. 3. Dynamic Questions: What is your primary source of
income? (Agriculture/Salary/Business), Do you have a current salary slip or IT return? 4. Benefits:
Provides the foundational credential (Certificate) required for schemes like Fee Reimbursement and
Housing. 5. Required Documents: Aadhaar (Verified), Ration Card/EPIC (Verified), Salary Certificate
/ IT Returns / Affidavit (Manual Upload). 6. Application Procedure: Citizen applies via MeeSeva/e-
District → Forwarded to VRO/RI → Physical Enquiry (if required) → Tahsildar Approval → Digital
Certificate Issued. 7. Fees & Processing Time: Typically ₹35 to ₹50 service charge at MeeSeva.
SLA is usually 7 to 15 working days. 8. Status Flow: Submitted → Assigned to VRO → VRO
Approved → Assigned to Tahsildar → Digitally Signed & Issued. 9. Rejection Reasons: Incomplete
documents, mismatch between declared income and field inquiry, fake affidavits. 10. Related
Entities: If Income Certificate issued < ₹2,00,000 → Unlocks Education Scholarships and Health
Schemes.

16. Disability Pension / SADAREM (Pension - State)
1. Basic Information
 Scheme Name: Aasara Disability Pension.
 Type: State Government (Telangana).
 Department: Society for Elimination of Rural Poverty (SERP).
 Objective: To provide financial security to persons with disabilities (PwDs).
 Target Beneficiaries: Disabled citizens from BPL families.
2. Eligibility
 Age Limit: No age limit for disability pensions.
 Disability Percentage: Must have a minimum of 40% disability (51% for hearing impaired)
certified by SADAREM (Software for Assessment of Disabled for Access Rehabilitation and
Empowerment).
 Income Limit: Must be from a Below Poverty Line (BPL) household.
3. Dynamic Eligibility Questions (System Logic)
 Do you have a valid SADAREM Certificate? (Maps to boolean: has_sadarem_cert)

disability_percentage)
 Do you have a White Ration Card? (Maps to boolean: has_white_card)
4. Benefits
 Financial Assistance: Enhanced monthly pension (currently around ₹3,016 or as updated
per latest GOs), credited via DBT.
5. Required Documents
Document Name Requirement Source/Platform Manual Upload?
SADAREM Certificate Mandatory SADAREM DB API No
Aadhaar Card Mandatory eKYC No
BPL/White Ration Card Mandatory Civil Supplies API No
Bank Account Mandatory NPCI No
6. Application Procedure
1. Certification: Citizen attends a medical board assessment to get the SADAREM certificate.
2. Application: Submits pension application at MeeSeva or Gram Panchayat.
3. Field Enquiry: Panchayat Secretary validates the BPL status.
4. Approval: MPDO sanctions the pension based on the SADAREM ID validation.
